ABOUT US

History and Background

Established in the year 1986, "R.R.CHEMICALS & INDUSTRIES PRIVATE LIMITED" is engaged in manufacturing and supplying an assortment type of Textile Speciality Chemicals and allied products - Antistatic Lubricants, Dyeing Additives etc. Our Mr. R.R.Mehta, Degree in Textile Chemistry Engineering with vast experience as Dyeing Supervisor to Dyeing Manager with various reputed Textiles Mills all over India during the period 1968 to 1986 has started the unit in 1986

Having the hard earned working experience as well as the good academic background, our mentor Mr.R.R.Mehta we do have the added advantage of knowing the actual requirements of the textile industries and accordingly we received good accolades to our products and hence could make defect free supplies to all the clients from whom we received orders, and they are using our products repeatedly. We have achieved comprehensive professional success under the guidance of our mentor Mr. R.R.Mehta and Mr. Dhiraj R Mehta (Production & Marketing Director). The company started as small unit has subsequently shifted to new location having 15,000 sq. feet of area presently utilizing by the factory with another 30,000 sq. feet acquired for future expansion.

The presence of qualified, trained, dedicated and sincere work force/ technicians and field staff enable us to cater the demand of our various clients. The sound financial strength as well as the owned factory and office building enable us to conduct the operation to the complete satisfaction of our clients. Our unit has been registered under DIC, MIDC as well as under ISO 9001: 2008.

Our mentor Mr.R.R.Mehta is an active member of the Managing Committee of Textile Association (India) Mumbai unit from 1989 onwards and still being actively associated with the TAI's activity. Mr. R.R.Mehta was the Hon. Jt Secretary during the year 2003 to 2012. He has organized several programs of the Association and in appreciation of that is the recipient of special memento in the year 2012
